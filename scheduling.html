SCHEDULING INSTRUCTIONS

Request an appointment via ChARM PHR widget below! Start by choosing the appropriate appointment type, then scrolling down to see Dr. Hurley’s first available appointment dates/times.

If you are transferring care from Dr. Hurley’s previous clinic, select the “New Patient Telemed - Transfer of Care (45min)” appointment type; next click “No” to the question asking if you are an existing patient.

Once your appointment is accepted, you will receive an invitation to set up your CharmPHR account with instructions to complete intake paperwork along with details about your upcoming visit.

Having trouble finding your account or scheduling a visit? Please check the ChARM scheduling troubleshooting tips document here
