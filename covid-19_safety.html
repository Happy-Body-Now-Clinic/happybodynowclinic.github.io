COVID-19 SAFETY

Safety is top of mind when adventuring out during a global pandemic.

LIMITED IN PERSON CONTACT


Telemedicine appointments are preferred for all visits except for Well Visits which are performed in person.  If you or anyone in your household has traveled 14 days preceding your appointment, we ask that you schedule telehealth or postpone your in-person visit until two weeks have passed and everyone in the household has remained symptom-free. If you or anyone in your household is coughing, sneezing, or congested, or has a runny nose or fever, we ask that you convert to telehealth or reschedule your in-person visit 14 days after your last day experiencing any of these symptoms.
